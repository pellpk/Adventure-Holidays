Our Full-Day Adventure

Depending on your package, there will be many different activities.

Start your day with an exhilarating road cycling experience, exploring scenic routes and enjoying the fresh air.
For those who prefer mountain biking, tackle challenging trails and immerse yourself in nature.
If lake fishing is your choice, spend a peaceful morning by the water, casting your line and enjoying the tranquility.
Walking enthusiasts can embark on a guided hike, discovering breathtaking views and hidden gems.
Sailing offers a serene adventure, gliding across the water and soaking in the beauty of the surroundings.
Thrill-seekers can opt for base jumping, experiencing an adrenaline rush like no other.
White water rafting provides an exciting challenge, navigating rapids and working as a team.
Rock climbing is perfect for those who love heights and conquering natural rock formations.
Finally, paragliding lets you soar through the skies, taking in stunning aerial views and feeling the freedom of flight.

Midday, enjoy a thrilling lunch experience at our cliffside restaurant, where you can savor gourmet meals while taking in breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes. The combination of delicious food and stunning scenery will make your lunch truly unforgettable.

Price per person is £95.
